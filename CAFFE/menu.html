Drink Menu

Brewed Coffees

Every day we brew our house Colombian and French Roast Coffees as well as a rotating varietal.  For our full selection of coffees available brewed and whole-bean, go here…

Espresso Drinks

  • Espresso
  • Macchiato – Espresso with a dollop of foam.
  • Cortado – Espresso with 3oz of warm milk; short and smooth.
  • Cappuccino – One part espresso, one part milk, one part foam.
  • Au Lait – Brewed coffee and steamed milk.
  • Latte – Espresso and steamed milk, with a touch of foam.
  • Mocha – Espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate.

Cold Brewed Iced Coffee

    Available in both of our stores, and in growlers to go, We have cold brewed our iced coffee since 2001 to keep it smooth, chocolatey, and Cambridge’s best iced coffee.

Specialty Drinks

Hot Drinks

  • Homemade Chai – We make it in house with black tea, spices, a touch of sugar, and milk so its never too sweet and is just spicy enough.
  • Hot Cider – Each fall and winter, we source locally grown and pressed apple cider.
  • London Fog – A rich combination of steamed milk, earl grey tea, and vanilla.
  • Matcha – Japanese matcha powder, blended with milk. Available hot or cold.
  • Homemade Hot Chocolate – Blended in house with Dutch-process cocoa.
  • Mexican Hot Chocolate – Our house-made cocoa spiced with coriander and cinnamon.
  • Vietnamese Coffee – Available hot and iced, it’s a rich combination of dark roast and sweetened condensed milk.

Iced Drinks

  • Malted Cold Brew – Our concoction of concentrated cold brew, milk, cream, malt and a touch of sugar.
  • Lemonade – Always squeezed fresh to order.
  • Cranberry Cooler – A refreshing blend of 7-herb hibiscus tea and cranberry juice.
  • Iced Tea – In the summer time we offer up to six varieties of freshly brewed, unsweetened iced tea.
  • Italian Soda – Sweet syrups mixed with crisp sparkling water. Built to order. Make your own flavor!

Summertime Frozen Drinks

  • Mocha Slide – Our all-natural blended drink made with our concentrated cold brew, mik and chocolate!
